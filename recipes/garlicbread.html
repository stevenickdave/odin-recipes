Garlic Bread

Description

Make this classic side dish with a crispy crust and buttery, garlicky centre. Our cheesey garlic bread toasts are delicous served with lasagne, soups and plenty more.

Ingredients

  • 2 Ciabatta Loaves
  • 140 g Butter, softened
  • 4-6 Garlic Cloves
  • Handful of Chopped Parsley
  • Finely Grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Slice the bread in half lengthwise. Toast the crust side for 2 mins until really crispy. Mix together butter, garlic and parsley in a bowel. Spread over the cut bread halves. Sprinkle over the parmesan. Just before serving place on a baking sheet, then cook under a hot grill for 5 mins untl toasty and lightly golden. Let it cool for 1 min before cutting into slices
